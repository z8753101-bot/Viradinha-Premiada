How it works

Viradinha Premiada is a promotional raffle app where participants can purchase lucky numbers to compete for prizes drawn at the end of the year, similar to official lotteries.

Purpose

The app aims to provide a simple, transparent and fun raffle experience, respecting Brazilian regulations and keeping the rules clear.
